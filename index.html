Welcome to Maths Makes My Day.

  • Are you looking for help with maths and physics?
  • Is the next test or exam coming up and you don't feel prepared?
  • Has your child lost interest in maths and physics?
  • Do you enjoy maths and physics and want to learn more?
  • Would you like to discuss maths or physics in German?

Then you have found the right place.

Maths Makes My Day offers individual and small group tutoring to students in the following subjects:

  • Mathematics
  • Science
  • German

Students can choose between tutoring in person at home or nearby venue or tutoring online via Skype. Maths Makes My Day is based in the south eastern suburbs of Melbourne, Australia, and tutoring is available in the following locations:

  • Frankston
  • Dandenong
  • Cranbourne
  • and surrounding suburbs

Students have the choice of tutoring in either English or German.
