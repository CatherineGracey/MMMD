Tutoring

For all subjects I offer a home tutoring service for the Frankston, Dandenong and Cranbourne areas, and online tutoring sessions using Skype. Tutoring sessions are possible as the traditional one-on-one or in small groups with up to 5 students if you and your friends want to work through certain problems together.

My tutoring services include, but are not limited to, homework support, exam preparation, general understanding, and experiments.
